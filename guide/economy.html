Economy

To simulate real life, SandbloxMC operates under an item-based economy where 1 gold ingot is equivalent to $1.

Gold Dollars
1 Gold Ingot $1
1 Gold Block $9

Viewing balance

To check your account balance, type /bal or /balance.

Storing gold

Gold can be stored in an ender chest for safe keeping. If you are a town owner, you may choose to deposit your gold in your town's bank using the /t deposit (amount) command.

Earning gold

Gold can be earned in many ways:

  • Voting for the server with /vote
  • Mining
  • Trading with other players
  • Selling/buying items with player shops

Note: Gold drops from Zombified Piglins are disabled to prevent inflation.

Transferring gold

To transfer gold from your account to another player, type /pay (player name) (amount). Alternatively, you may simply transfer the gold directly to another player.
