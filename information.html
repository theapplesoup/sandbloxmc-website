Useful Information

Towny Commands

/town new (townname) - Create a new town for 64 gold ingots
/town invite (player) - Invite a player to your town
/town claim - Claim a chunk for 16 gold ingots
/town spawn - Teleport to your town's spawn
/town deposit ($) - Deposit gold into your town bank
/town withdraw ($) - Withdraw gold from your town bank

/nation new (townname) - Create a nation for 500 gold ingots
/nation spawn (nationname) - Teleport to another nation's spawn
/nation ally add (nationname) - Ally with another nation
/nation add (townname) - Invite a town to your nation

A complete list of towny commands can be found here.

Dynmap

Dynmap is a live interactive Google Earth style website for Minecraft servers. Click here to go to the map.

This map allows players to visualize each nation's territory and locate where they are on the map whenever they want. Nations are highlighted in blue. Towns without nations are highlighted in yellow. The star icon represents a nation's capital.

Currency

SandbloxMC uses gold ingots as an item-based currency. Gold is obtained by mining in the overworld and trading with other players. Nether Gold Ore generation is disabled. The conversion rates are as follows:

1 Gold Ingot = $1
1 Gold Block = $9

To store gold virtually, use the following commands:
/bank deposit ($) - Deposit gold into your bank account
/bank withdraw ($) - Withdraw gold from your bank account
/bank pay (player) ($) - Send gold to an online or offline player
/bank balance - Show your balance

Player Shops

To create a shop, simply left click on a chest holding the item that you'd like to buy/sell. Then, follow the instructions given in the chat.

Player shops must be created on townblocks set to "shop". To set a plot type to "shop", use the command /plot set shop on the chunk where you'd like to create a shop.
